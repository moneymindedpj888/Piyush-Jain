# 🛒 Superstore Sales Data Analysis Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview

The **Superstore Sales Dashboard** is a data analytics project designed to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, profit trends, and operational efficiency using interactive visualizations.
This dashboard provides business insights that help decision-makers understand **sales patterns, profitability, and regional performance**.

The project demonstrates practical skills in **data analysis, business intelligence, and dashboard design**.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze overall sales and profit performance
* Identify top-performing categories and subcategories
* Understand monthly sales and profit trends
* Compare shipping modes and payment methods
* Evaluate regional and state-wise performance
* Generate actionable business insights

---

## 📁 Dataset

The dataset used is the **Superstore Sales Dataset**, which includes:

* Order Date
* Sales
* Profit
* Category & Sub-category
* Customer Segment
* Payment Mode
* Shipping Mode
* Region & State
* Quantity Ordered

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### 1️⃣ Sales Analysis

* Monthly sales comparison (Year-wise)
* Sales distribution by customer segment
* Category-wise sales performance

### 2️⃣ Profit Analysis

* Monthly profit trends
* Profit contribution across regions
* Overall profitability metrics

### 3️⃣ Customer & Order Insights

* Payment mode analysis
* Total orders and quantity sold
* Consumer vs Corporate vs Home Office comparison

### 4️⃣ Logistics Insights

* Shipping mode performance
* Average shipping days analysis

### 5️⃣ Regional Analysis

* Interactive state-level sales visualization
* Region filters (Central, East, South, West)

## 🔍 Key Insights

* **Consumer segment** contributes the highest share of sales.
* **COD payment mode** dominates transactions.
* **Office Supplies** category generates the highest revenue.
* Sales peak during mid-year months indicating seasonal demand.
* Standard shipping is the most commonly used delivery method.
* Certain states significantly outperform others in sales contribution.
